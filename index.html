Planning advice

Key Tips for
Your Group

Things to know before you book ‚Äî especially with toddlers and a mixed group.

01 üìÖ

Book EARLY ‚Äî 18+ Months Ahead

Disney 2028 bookings open ~2026. For a group of 9 with specific cabin needs, book the day bookings open. Nursery reservations open separately and fill within hours ‚Äî don't miss this!

02 üõèÔ∏è

Cabin Strategy for Your Group

Disney staterooms are designed for families and are larger than industry average. Consider a connecting cabin or concierge suite for the toddler families. Adults without kids can book standard verandah cabins nearby.

03 üçº

Toddler Nursery ‚Äî Reserve Immediately

The "it's a small world" nursery has very limited spots. You must book nursing sessions as soon as the window opens. Bring your own supplies. There's an hourly fee (~$9‚Äì$11/hour per child).

04 üç∑

Bring Your Own Wine / Beer

Disney allows guests to bring 2 bottles of wine or 6 beers per adult at embarkation. Bring good bottles ‚Äî corkage at Palo is worth it! This is a great way to save on drinks costs.

05 üí∞

Budget Planning

5-night Caribbean cruise in February: expect ~$800‚Äì$2,000+ per person depending on cabin type. Budget separately for: specialty dining, nursery fees, alcohol, excursions, spa & gratuities ($16/day per person).

06 üé≠

Look for Special Sailings

In February, watch for Marvel Day at Sea on the Disney Magic (from Galveston) or special themed sailings. These add incredible bonus entertainment for all ages ‚Äî superheroes, exclusive merchandise & experiences.
